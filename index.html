Southern Fashion

SOURTH was born in Oregon State in 2021. Southern fashion owns 4 reowned women’s wear brands.Unque high-street SOURTH, affordable luxury DIAMOND SOURTH, mixed-up chic S'OURTH, star-crossover brand NA BY SOURTH. SOURTH has been rather clear of its positioning. By the end of 1year born, SOURTH Fashion had have more than 50 stores around the nation. in a long tern of concern, SOURTH Fashion will focus on care value construction which leads to fashion-themed park. To be brave, dare to dream, to shine, to stand out on international fashion stage.
